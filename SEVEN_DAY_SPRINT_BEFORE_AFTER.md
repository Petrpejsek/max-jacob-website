## Design Psychology

**Why 4 steps works better:**
1. **Process credibility:** 4 steps = still thorough enough
2. **Cognitive load:** 4 is easier to remember than 7
3. **Visual hierarchy:** Title â†’ promise â†’ bullets = clear layers
4. **Deliverable consistency:** Each step has exactly 2 bullets
5. **No overwhelm:** Client sees progress points without fatigue

**Why the new format works:**
- **Title:** Quick understanding of phase
- **Promise:** Immediate value ("what you get")
- **Bullets:** Specific deliverables (scannable)
